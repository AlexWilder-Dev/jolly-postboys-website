About Us

A proper community pub

Florence Park, Oxford

Your Local

The Jolly Postboys is an independent, family-run community pub in the heart of Florence Park, Oxford. We specialise in local and craft beers, with a menu of pub classics done properly.

We're a proper local — a pub that knows its regulars, welcomes new faces, and takes pride in being part of the Florence Park community. Whether you're popping in for a pint or settling in for Sunday lunch, you're always welcome.

Our tap selection showcases the best of the region's independent breweries alongside crowd-pleasing classics. The kitchen focuses on honest pub food — nothing fancy, just good ingredients cooked well.

Dogs welcome, kids welcome, and there's a great beer garden for when the Oxford sun shows up.

Allergen Information

Your safety matters to us

The Jolly Postboys takes food allergies and intolerances seriously. Under the Food Information Regulation 2014, we are required to provide allergen information for all dishes on our menu.

If you have a food allergy or intolerance, please speak to a member of staff before ordering. We will be happy to advise on suitable dishes and can check specific ingredients for you.

Our kitchen handles all 14 major allergens. While we take every precaution, we cannot guarantee that any dish is completely free from allergens due to the risk of cross-contamination in a busy kitchen environment.

The 14 Major Allergens

The following allergens are required by UK law to be declared when used as an ingredient:

🌾

Cereals containing Gluten

Wheat, rye, barley, oats and their hybridised strains.

🦐

Crustaceans

Prawns, crabs, lobster, crayfish.

🥚

Eggs

All products containing eggs.

🐟

Fish

All species of fish and fish products.

🥜

Peanuts

Peanuts and peanut-derived products.

🫘

Soybeans

Soya and soya-based products.

🥛

Milk

Milk and dairy products including lactose.

🌰

Nuts

Almonds, hazelnuts, walnuts, cashews, pecans, Brazil nuts, pistachios, macadamia nuts.

🌿

Celery

Celery and celeriac including seeds and leaves.

🟡

Mustard

Mustard plant, seeds, leaves, powder and paste.

🌰

Sesame Seeds

Sesame seeds and sesame oil.

🍷

Sulphur Dioxide & Sulphites

Present in wine, beer, dried fruits and some preserved foods at concentrations >10mg/kg.

🌸

Lupin

Lupin flour and seeds, sometimes used in bread, pastries and pasta.

🐚

Molluscs

Squid, mussels, oysters, scallops, snails and similar.

Questions? Talk to Us.

Our staff are trained to help with allergen queries. Before you order, please let us know about any allergies or intolerances — we would rather take the time to check than risk your health.
